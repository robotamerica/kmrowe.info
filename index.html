km. rowe is a geographer, educator, and multidisciplinary artist based in Xalapa, Mexico. Embracing the principle of festina lente (make haste slowly), km. integrates diligence, balance, and tranquility into his work. He identifies as an anarchist, eschewing hierarchical structures in favor of collaborative and decentralized approaches to life and work.
